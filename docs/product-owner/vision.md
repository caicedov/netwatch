# Product Vision

## Problem Statement

This game addresses the desire for immersive, strategic gameplay in a hacking simulation, allowing players to engage in real-time hacking scenarios that challenge their skills and creativity.

## Long-term Engagement

The game offers continuous updates, evolving challenges, and community-driven content, ensuring players remain engaged over time.

## Differentiators

Unlike other games, this title combines real-time multiplayer interactions with a focus on hacking strategy, providing a unique blend of competition and collaboration.

---

# MVP Scope Definition

## Features IN (Must-Have for MVP)

1. Real-time multiplayer hacking mechanics
2. Basic hacking tools and strategies
3. Player profiles and progression systems
4. Server-authoritative gameplay to ensure fairness

## Features OUT (Intentionally Excluded)

1. Advanced hacking tools (to be added in future updates)
2. Extensive customization options for characters
3. In-depth story mode

## Prioritization Order for IN Features

1. Real-time multiplayer mechanics
2. Basic hacking tools
3. Player profiles
4. Server authority

## Reasoning for Exclusions

Advanced tools and customization can complicate initial development and dilute focus on core gameplay mechanics.

---

# Success Metrics

- Player retention rates after 30 days
- Number of active players in the first month
- Engagement metrics (average session length, frequency of play)

---

# Risks & Unknowns

## Product Risks

- Engagement: Players may lose interest if content updates are slow.
- Retention: High competition in the gaming market may affect player retention.

## Market Risks

- Niche Size: The appeal of hacking games may be limited.

## Technical Unknowns

- Scalability: Uncertainty about how well the game will handle high concurrency.
