# Risks & Unknowns

## Product Risks

- Engagement: Risk of players losing interest if content updates are infrequent.
- Retention: High competition in the gaming market may impact player retention.

## Market Risks

- Niche Size: The appeal of hacking games may be limited, affecting market size.

## Technical Unknowns

- Scalability: Uncertainty regarding the game's ability to handle high concurrency.

## Mitigation Strategies

- Regular content updates to maintain player interest.
- Market research to understand player preferences and adjust accordingly.
